Q1 Menene 5 + 3?
a) 8
b) 7
c) 6
d) 9
Bayani: Bugu da kari ya shafi hada lambobi biyu ko fiye don gano jimlar. Don warware 5 + 3, ka ƙidaya 5, sannan ka ƙidaya ƙarin 3, wanda daidai amsar 8. Don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 8.
Q2 Idan kuna da apples 4 kuma kuna samun ƙarin 2, apples nawa kuke da su gaba ɗaya?
a) 4
b) 5
c) 6
d) 7
Bayani: Don gano yawan apples da kuke da su gaba ɗaya, zaku ƙara adadin apples da kuka fara da (4) zuwa adadin apples da kuka karɓa (2). Don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 6.
Q3 Wanne daga cikin waɗannan iri ɗaya ne kamar 2 + 6?
a) 5 + 2
b) 6 + 3
c) 7 + 4
d) 4 + 4 
Bayanin: 4 + 4 iri ɗaya ne kamar 2 + 6. Counting 4 daga 4 yana bamu 8. Don haka, amsar da ta dace ita ce d.
Q4 idan kuna da alewa 7 kuma kuna cin 2 daga cikinsu, da yawa alumma kuka bar?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Bayani: Lokacin da kuka ci wani abu, kuna da ƙarancin abin. Don haka, don gano nawa ne ƙides da kuka bari, kuna cire adadin albiɗɗan da aka ci (2) daga adadin alewai (7). 7 - 2 daidai 5. Saboda haka, amsar daidai ita ce 5.
Q5 Menene lambar da ta ɓace a cikin daidaituwa: 9 + __ = 11?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 4
Bayani: Don nemo lambar da aka rasa, kun gano nawa 11 na shekara 11. Saboda haka, lambar da aka ɓace shine 2, tun ƙarfe 2. Saboda haka, amsar da ta dace ita ce 2.
Q6 Menene 9 - 3?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Bayani: Kadan ya ƙunshi kwantar da lamba ɗaya daga wani. Don warware 9 - 3, za ku fara da 9 kuma ku tafi da 3, ya bar ku da 6. haka, amsar daidai ita ce 6.
Q7 Idan kana da dabbobi 7 kuma ka ba da 2, da yawa marbles ka bar?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Bayanin: Lokacin da kuka ba da wani abu, kuna da ƙarancin abin. Don haka, don gano yawan marbles da kuka bari, kuna cire lambar da aka basu (2) daga adadin managbes (7). 7 - 2 daidai 5. Saboda haka, amsar daidai ita ce 5.
Q8 Wanne daga cikin waɗannan iri ɗaya ne da 8 - 4?
a) 7 - 3
b) 6 - 2
c) 5 - 3
d) 8 - 2
Bayanin: 7 - 3 daidai yake da 8 - 4. 8 Cire 4 yana ba mu 4, 7 Cire 3 kuma yana ba mu 4 kuma yana ba mu amsar daidai.
Q9 Idan akwai tsuntsaye 10 a kan itace da 3 tashi, da yawa daga cikin itacen?
a) 5
b) 6
c) 7
d) 8
Bayani: Lokacin da tsuntsaye su tashi, jimlar tsuntsaye ke raguwa. Don gano yawan tsuntsaye nawa, kuna cire lambar da ta tashi (3) daga adadin tsuntsaye (10). 10 - 3 daidai 7. Don haka, amsar da ta dace shine 7.
Q10 Menene lambar da aka rasa a cikin daidaituwa: 6 - __ = 4?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 4
Bayani: Don nemo lambar da aka rasa, kuna cire sakamakon da aka sani daga lambar farawa. Don haka, 6 - 4 daidai yake 2. Don haka, lambar da aka ɓace shine 2 don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 2.
Q11 Menene 4 x 3?
a) 6
b) 7
c) 10
d) 12
Bayani: Mai ninka ya shafi ƙarin ƙari. Don magance 4 x 3, zaka ƙara 4 sau uku, 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 = 12. Don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 12.
Q12 Idan akwai fensir 5 a kowane fakitoci 4 fakitoci, nawa fencils suke a cikin duka?
a) 20
b) 15
c) 36
d) 25
Bayani: Don gano yadda alkaluman fensir nawa suke a gaba ɗaya, zaku ninka adadin fensir a cikin kowane fakitin (5) ta adadin fakitoci (4). 5 x 4 daidai da 20. Saboda haka, amsar da ta dace shine 20.
Q13 Wanne daga cikin waɗannan iri ɗaya ne kamar 3 x 6?
a) 9 x 2
b) 12 x 3
c) 4 x 4
d) 7 x 2
Bayanin: 3 Jigilar 6 ya bamu 18, 9 sun ninka da 2 kuma, amsar daidai ita ce.
Q14 Idan akwai ɗalibai 8 a kowane layi kuma akwai layuka 3, ɗalibai nawa ne a gabaɗaya?
a) 16
b) 20
c) 24
d) 32
Bayani: Don gano nawa ɗalibai suke a gabaɗaya, kuna ninka yawan ɗalibai a cikin kowane jere (8) ta yawan layuka (3). 8 x 3 daidai 24. Saboda haka, amsar daidai ita ce 24.
Q15 Menene lambar da ta ɓace a cikin daidaituwa: 7 x __ = 21?
a) 4
b) 3
c) 2
d) 1
Bayani: Don nemo lambar da aka rasa, raba sakamakon da lambar farawa. Don haka, 21 ya raba maki 7 daidai uku 3. Saboda haka, lambar da aka rasa ita ce 3. haka, amsar da ta dace shine 3.
Q16 Menene 12 raba ta 3?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 6
Bayanin: Divist shine akasin aikin ninka na yawa. Don warware 12 rarraba ta 3, kuna buƙatar gano ɗakuna uku na 3 a cikin 12. Tun daga 12 za'a iya raba shi zuwa rukuni 4 na 3. Don haka, amsar ita ce 4.
Q17 Idan akwai alewa 15 kuma ana rabawa daidai tsakanin abokai 5, alsiya nawa kowannensu yake samu?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
Bayanin: Divation ne game da rabawa daidai. Don gano sau nawa aboki kowannensu ya samu, kuna raba adadin alewa (15) da yawan abokai (5). 15 Raba da 5 daidai 3. Don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 3.
Q18 Wanne daga cikin waɗannan daidai ne kamar 20 rarraba ta 4?
a) 100 rarraba ta 25
b) 50 raba da 10
c) 6 Raba 3
d) 7 raba 1
Bayanin: Rarraba na kishiyar yawa. Don haka, don gano abin da 20 raba da 4 daidai da kungiyoyi 4 ya shiga 20. Don haka, sakamakon shine 5 yana ba mu 5. Don haka, amsar ita ce ta 10 tana ba mu 5. saboda haka, amsar ita ce ta 10 tana ba mu 5. saboda haka, amsar ita ce, don haka, amsar ita ce ta 10 yana ba mu 5. saboda haka, amsar ita ce, don haka, amsar ita ce
Q19 Idan akwai apples 24 kuma ana sanya su cikin kwanduna 6 daidai, da yawa apples suke a cikin kowane kwandon?
a) 2
b) 4
c) 6
d) 8
Bayanin: Divation ne game da rabawa daidai. Don gano apples nawa ne a cikin kowane kwandon, kuna raba jimlar apples (24) ta yawan kwanduna (6). 24 Rarraba ta 6 daidai 4. Don haka, amsar da ta dace shine 4.
Q20 Menene lambar da ta ɓace a cikin daidaituwa: 15 rarraba ta __ = 3?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
Bayani: Don nemo lambar da aka rasa, ka rarraba sakamakon da amsar ta amsa. Don haka, 15 ya raba 3 daidai da 3 saboda haka, lambar da aka rasa ita ce 5. Don haka, amsar daidai ita ce 5.
