Q1 Chii chinonzi 5 + 3?
a) 8
B) 7
c) 6
d) 9
Tsananguro: Kuwedzera kunosanganisira kusanganisa manhamba maviri kana anopfuura kuti uwane iyo yakazara. Kugadzirisa 5 + 3, iwe unoverenga 5, wozoverenga 3 zvimwe, izvo zvakaenzana 8. Saizvozvo, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye8.
Q2 Kana iwe uine maapuro mana uye iwe unowana 2 zvimwe, maapuro mangani aunawo zvachose?
a) 4
b) 5
c) 6
d) 7
Tsananguro: Kuti uzive kuti maapuro mangani aunawo, iwe unowedzera huwandu hwemapapu aunotanga na (4) kune nhamba yemaapuro iwe yawakagamuchira (2). Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye6.
Q3 Ndeupi pane anotevera akafanana ne2 2 + 6?
a) 5 + 2
b) 6 + 3
c) 7 + 4
d) 4 + 4 
Tsananguro: 4 + 4 yakafanana ne2 2 + 6. Kuverenga 4 kubva 4 kunotipa 8. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye d.
Q4 Kana iwe uine mamirioni manomwe uye iwe unodya 2 ivo, iwe wasara makenduru?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Tsananguro: Paunodya chimwe chinhu, iwe une mashoma pachinhu icho. Nekudaro, kuti uwane kuti mangani makiromita aunaye, iwe unobvisa huwandu hwemapipi hwakadyiwa (2) kubva kune huwandu hwakazara hwemamendi (7). 7 - 2 yakaenzana 5. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 5.

Q6 Chii 9 - 3?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Tsananguro: Kubvisa pasi kunosanganisira kubvisa imwe nhamba kubva kune imwe. Kugadzirisa 9 - 3, iwe unotanga ne9 uye utore 3, uchisiya iwe uine 6. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 6.
Q7 Kana iwe uine 7 marble uye iwe unopa 2, mangangarini mangani?
a) 3
b) 4
c) 5
d) 6
Tsananguro: Paunopa chimwe chinhu kure, iwe une zvishoma chinhu icho. Nekudaro, kuti uzive kuti mangani marbles iwe wawakasiya, iwe unobvisa nhamba yakapihwa kure (2) kubva kune huwandu hwehuwandu hwemarble (7). 7 - 2 yakaenzana 5. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 5.
Q8 Ndeupi pane anotevera akafanana ne8 - 4?
a) 7 - 3
B) 6 - 2
c) 5 - 3
d) 8 - 2
Tsananguro: 7 - 3 yakafanana ne8 - 4. 8 Tora 4 inotipa 4, 7 tora 3 inotipa zvakare inotipa 4, saka mhinduro kwayo ndeye.
Q9 Kana paine shiri gumi pamuti uye 3 kubhururuka kure, kushingairira kwakawanda kusiiwa pamuti?
a) 5
b) 6
c) 7
d) 8
Tsananguro: Kana shiri dzinobhururuka, huwandu hweshiri dzinodzikira. Kuti uzive kuti vangani shiri dzasara, iwe unobvisa iyo nhamba iyo yakabhururuka ichienda (3) kubva kuhuwandu hweshiri (10). 10 - 3 zvakaenzana 7. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 7.
Q10 Ndeipi nhamba yakarasika muEquation: 6 - __ = 4?
a) 1
b) 2
c) 3
d) 4
Tsananguro: Kuti uwane iyo nhamba yakarasika, iwe unobvisa iyo inozivikanwa mhedzisiro kubva kune nhamba yekutanga. Saka, 6 - 4 yakaenzana 2. Naizvozvo, nhamba yakarasika ndeye 2. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 2.
Q11 Chii chinonzi 4 x 3?
a) 6
B) 7
c) 10
d) 12
Tsananguro: Kuwedzera kunosanganisira kudzokorora kumwezve. Kugadzirisa 4 x 3, iwe unowedzera 4 katatu, 4 + 4 + 4 + 4 = 12. Saka mhinduro kwayo ndeyegumi nemaviri.
Q12 Kana paine mapenzura mashanu mune rimwe nerimwe paki uye pane mana mapaketi, mangani mapenzura aripo akazara?
a) 20
b) 15
c) 36
d) 25
Tsananguro: Kuti uzive kuti mangani mapenzura aripo akazara, iwe unowanzura huwandu hwemapenzura muhomwe imwe neimwe (5) nehuwandu hwemapaketi (4). 5 x 4 yakaenzana 20. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye makumi maviri.
Q31 Ndeipi yeinotevera yakafanana ne3 x 6?
a) 9 x 2
b) 12 x 3
c) 4 x 4
d) 7 x 2
Tsananguro: 3 Mutliped ne6 inotipa 18, 9 yakawedzera ne2 inotipa zvakare 18. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye.
Q14 Kana paine vadzidzi vasere mumutsara wega wega uye kune matatu mitsara, vangani vadzidzi varipo?
a) 16
b) 20
c) 24
d) 32
Tsananguro: Kuti uzive kuti vangani vadzidzi variko mune yakazara, iwe unowanzwanza nhamba yevadzidzi mumutsara wega wega (8) nehuwandu hwemitsara (3). 8 x 3 yakaenzana 24. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 24.
Q15 Ndeipi iyo iyo nhamba isipo muEquation: 7 x __ = 21?
a) 4
b) 3
c) 2
d) 1
Tsananguro: Kuti uwane iyo nhamba yakarasika, iwe unokamura zvinozivikanwa mhedzisiro neyekutanga nhamba. Saka, 21 yakakamurwa ne7 yakaenzana 3. Saka, nhamba yakarasika ndeye 3. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 3.
Q16 Chii chinonzi 12 chakakamurwa ne3?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 6
Tsananguro: Kurudziro ndiko kuvhiringidza kwekuwedzera. Kugadzirisa gumi nemaviri akapatsanurwa na3, iwe unofanirwa kuziva kuti mangani mapoka e3 aripo muna 12. Kubva gumi nemaviri anogona kukamurwa kuita mapoka mana e 3. Saka, mhinduro ndeye 4.
Q17 Kana paine mamirioni gumi nemashanu uye iwo akagovaniswa zvakaenzana pakati peshamwari shanu, shamwari imwe neimwe inowana marii?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
Tsananguro: Chikamu ndechekuda kugovana zvakafanana. Kuti uzive kuti mangani emigadziro shamwari imwe neimwe, iwe unokamura huwandu hwemamendi (15) nehuwandu hweshamwari (5). 15 Yakakamurwa ne5 yakaenzana 3. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 3.
Q18 Ndeipi yeinotevera yakafanana ne20 yakakamurwa na4?
a) 100 yakakamurwa ne25
b) 50 yakakamurwa negumi
c) 6 yakakamurwa ne3
d) 7 yakakamurwa ne1
Tsananguro: Chikamu chakapesana nekuwanda. Nekudaro, kuti uzive kuti 20 zvakakamurwa makumi maviri nemairi akaenzana, unogona kufunga nezve mapoka e4 anopinda muna 20. Saka, mhedzisiro ndeye 5, 50 yakakamurwa negumi inotipa 5. Saka, mhinduro iri b
Q19 Kana paine maapuro makumi maviri neshanu uye iwo akaiswa mumabhasikiti matanhatu zvakaenzana, maapuro mangani aripo mudengu rimwe nerimwe?
a) 2
b) 4
c) 6
d) 8
Tsananguro: Chikamu ndechekuda kugovana zvakafanana. Kuti uwane kuti maapuro mangani aripo mudengu rimwe nerimwe, unokamura huwandu hwemaapuro (24) nehuwandu hwemabhasikiti (6). 24 Yakakamurwa ne6 yakaenzana 4. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 4.
Q20 Ndeipi nhamba yakarasika mu equation: 15 yakakamurwa ne __ = 3?
a) 2
b) 3
c) 4
d) 5
Tsananguro: Kuti uwane iyo nhamba yakarasika, iwe unokamura mhedzisiro inozivikanwa nemhinduro. Saka, 15 yakakamurwa ne3 yakaenzana 5. Naizvozvo, nhamba yakarasika ndeye 5. Saka, mhinduro chaiyo ndeye 5.
